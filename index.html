My Methodology

FAQ

How do online lessons work?

We meet on Zoom or Google Meet. I share materials during class through Google Docs; you'll have your own folder with all corrections so you can review whenever you like, and I send follow-up notes afterwards.

Can I reschedule a lesson?

Yes — just give me 24 hours’ notice and we’ll move it.

Do you teach beginners?

Absolutely! I have structured courses for CEFR A1–C2 levels.

How fast will I improve?

Most students move ½ CEFR level every 6–8 weeks on Starter and a full level on Regular or Intensive—provided you do the homework.

Do you guarantee exam scores?

Complete all tasks and mock tests; if you miss your target band, I refund the last two lessons.

What tech do we need?

A quiet corner, decent Wi-Fi and either Zoom or Google Meet. All materials arrive as PDFs or Google Docs.
